## Introduction to Cybersecurity – Key Concepts

- The main goal of cybersecurity is to maintain **confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA) of information**.

- Entry-level security analysts typically:
  - Protect organizational systems and data
  - Look for vulnerabilities and security gaps
  - Continuously monitor systems for suspicious activity

- Reviewing system activity, logs, and organizational records is known as a **security audit**.

- Cybersecurity teams add value by:
  - Defending against both internal and external threats
  - Helping organizations meet legal and regulatory requirements


## Core Skills for Cybersecurity Professionals

- Important transferable skills include:
  - Analytical thinking
  - Written and verbal communication
  - Problem-solving ability

- Technical skills often include:
  - Programming knowledge
  - Data analysis skills

- Professionals who investigate cyber incidents and handle digital evidence are called:
  - Digital forensic investigators

- Examples of highly sensitive personal information (SPII) include:
  - Financial data like bank account numbers
  - Personal medical records
