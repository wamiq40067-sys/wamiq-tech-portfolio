# Foundations of Cybersecurity

This folder contains my learning progress, notes, assignments, screenshots, and reflections from the first course of the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate: **Foundations of Cybersecurity**.

## Course Overview

This course introduced the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity, including how organizations protect systems, networks, and sensitive information from cyber threats. It also provided an introduction to security principles, threat actors, risk management, and the role of cybersecurity professionals in modern organizations.

## Skills and Concepts Learned

- Cybersecurity fundamentals
- CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability)
- Types of cyber threats and attacks
- Threat actors and motivations
- Security domains
- Risk management basics
- Security frameworks and controls
- Phishing awareness
- Importance of organizational security

## Activities Completed

- Graded assignments
- Practice activities
- Security awareness exercises
- Phishing identification tasks
- Reflection exercises
