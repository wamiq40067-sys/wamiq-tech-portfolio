# Put It to Work: Prepare for Cybersecurity Jobs

This file contains my learning notes, key concepts, and understanding of cybersecurity career preparation, professional communication, incident escalation, ethical practices, and job readiness as part of the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate.

---

## Topic Overview

This course focuses on developing the professional skills required for a successful cybersecurity career. It covers incident escalation procedures, communication with stakeholders, ethical decision-making, confidentiality, career development, professional networking, and job application preparation.

The course also explores how cybersecurity professionals collaborate with teams, communicate security incidents effectively, stay informed about emerging threats, engage with the cybersecurity community, and prepare for cybersecurity job interviews. By the end of the course, learners gain practical knowledge for transitioning from cybersecurity education into the professional workforce.
