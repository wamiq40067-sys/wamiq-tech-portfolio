## Key Topics Covered

### Threats, Risks, and Vulnerabilities
- Threats are potential causes of harm  
- Vulnerabilities are system weaknesses  
- Risk is the possibility of exploitation  
- Risk depends on likelihood and impact  

### Risk Management Approaches
- Acceptance: Accepting risk as it is  
- Avoidance: Removing risky activity  
- Mitigation: Reducing impact or likelihood  
- Transfer: Passing risk to third party  

### CISSP Security Domains (Overview)
- Security & Risk Management  
- Asset Security  
- Security Architecture & Engineering  
- Communication & Network Security  
- Identity & Access Management  
- Security Assessment & Testing  
- Security Operations  
- Software Development Security
